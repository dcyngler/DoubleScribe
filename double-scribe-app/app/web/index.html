Before you record

Double Scribe transcribes whatever your microphone and speakers pick up. It's your responsibility, not the app's, to tell everyone else on the call that you're transcribing, and to get their okay first — many places (including several US states, the UK, the EU, Canada, and Australia) legally require it, and other countries may too, so check the rules where you and the other participants are located.

Double Scribe doesn't join the call or post a bot/banner the way cloud notetakers do, so there's no automatic indicator for the other participants — saying it out loud, every time, is the only way they'll know.

Welcome to Double Scribe

Double Scribe transcribes your calls entirely on this computer — audio and transcripts never leave your machine.

While you're listening, your side of the conversation appears as Me, and everyone else appears as Them, right as it's said.

A fully-offline desktop based transcription app for virtual meetings on Windows.

Doesn't matter if the transcribe button belongs to the host, or the other side's on a completely different platform. Once everyone's agreed you can record, that's your cue to start Double Scribe, not before.

  • It sits on your own computers desktop
  • It transcribes live, while the call is happening (it auto detects the microphone input and speaker output)
  • It works in the background and you can end the transcription at any time

Free

Transcription shouldn't be a paywall. Double Scribe is free to download and free to use, with no subscription and no limits.

Private

Everything runs locally with Whisper, on your own CPU or GPU. Audio is held in memory only while you're recording, then discarded. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is recorded to disk.

Live

Watch the transcript appear while you're still talking, not after you hang up. Your mic and your speakers are automatically labelled Me and Them.

Simple

One toggle. Click start, talk, click stop. No setup, no configuration, no account to create.

Recording consent — know what applies to your call before you hit start.
